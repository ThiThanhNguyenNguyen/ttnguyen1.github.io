About us

We believe that tea has an ability to build a healthier and more altruistic life. Through enjoying a cup of tea, we are more mindful of the present moment, and be more grateful of what life is giving us. With the desire to bring values and happiness to every one, we chose tea to be our pride, our mission and our destiny. We travel the world, countryside to countryside, have a taste of hundreds of kinds of tea every month, with the only goal: bringing to you only the best. More than that, we also design great gift ideas and packaging to help you to connect with your loved ones.

Our head office is 2200 Sheppard East Scarborough, Ontario, J2J K9K, Canada

Our customer service hours of operation are 9h00 to 17h00.

Careers

Store Manager

The Store Manager will run the branch on a daily basis with targets for overall sales. You will be working with customers filling orders while performing general warehouse duties in our warehouse. It is a fast-paced, fun and you will lead a great team within the store. You will also have the benefit and support of your colleagues across our network.

Web developer

Under the direction of E-Commerce team, the Web Developer will be responsible for assisting with a variety of our marketing and development efforts, including rebuilding our existing sites, setting up our mobile app and more!


Shipping

We offer free standard shipping within Canada for orders of $50 or more and a flat fee of $9.75 for orders less than $50. Please allow at least 2 business days for your order to be processed. We do not process order on Canadian Statutory holidays or weekend.

Return Policy

We hope you like shopping at N& tea. Not satisfied with your latest purchase? We will gladly accept your return or exchange request for unused items within 30 days of the original receipt. We offer full refunds and exchanges on unused tea accessories, and in-store exchange or store credit on unused loose-leaf tea, food or sweeteners.

FAQ

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Our system is designed to fulfill orders quickly to make sure you receive them as soon as possible. This means that once an order is placed, we cannot cancel it, modify any of the items purchased, or change the shipping or billing address.

How do I talk to customer service?

You can call us at 416-254-6770 or email us at ntea@gmail.com. Our customer service hours of operation are 9h00 to 17h00.
