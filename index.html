BLACK TEA

Black tea is made by oxidizing and drying out green tea. The process changed the color of the tea leaf from green to black. When putting it in water, the tea leaf will make the tea become black or dark red hence the name of black tea. It has the strongest flavor and caffein proportion among all teas. Besides being a favorite drink, it could be used in many other recipes such as cakes, bubble milk tea…It is best used in the morning or at noon due to its energizing nature.
